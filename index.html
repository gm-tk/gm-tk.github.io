Why “ParseMaster”?

To parse means to divide a sentence into its grammatical parts and identify how they relate to each other — or, more broadly, to examine something in minute detail and analyse it critically. That’s exactly what this tool does: it takes the complex, layered XML structure of a .docx Writer Template — with its tracked changes, formatting runs, nested containers, and red-text annotations — and methodically breaks it down into clean, structured, labelled output ready for conversion. ParseMaster doesn’t just extract text; it parses every element, preserving the relationships between tags, content, and formatting that make accurate HTML conversion possible.
